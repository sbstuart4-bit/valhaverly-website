The Complete Narrative

Six chapters. One family. Zero arguments.

The recommended story flow — designed to take a prospect from recognition to conviction in under five minutes.

01

Meet Paul

Owns the family lake house — Dossey, Muskoka

  • Family scheduling is chaotic every spring
  • Creates a Valhaverly Circle in 20 minutes

"Every owner knows this feeling. Valhaverly gives Paul his weekends back."

02

Add Family Members

Sarah, James, Claire, and Emily join the circle

  • Everyone starts at Fairness Score 50
  • Token allocation: 2 prime · 1 holiday each

"A clean slate. No history. No politics. Just five people who love the same place."

03

Sarah Requests July Long Weekend

Prime weekend booking — 1 token consumed

  • Fairness score adjusts automatically: 50 → 33
  • James, Claire, Emily see the calendar update instantly

"She didn't have to ask anyone. The system said yes — and recorded it fairly."

04

James Requests August Week

James's score is 50 — higher than Sarah's 33

  • Wave 1 priority: James books first next season
  • Conflict never happens — the system handled it

"James didn't fight for it. He earned it by not going first last time."

05

Contributions & Shared Expenses

Stay contribution payment via Stripe

  • Circle wallet ledger updates in real time
  • Every member sees exactly what they owe and why

"The awkward money conversation — solved. Permanently."

06

Legacy & Stewardship

Years of family history preserved in one place

  • Multiple generations — scores, stays, contributions
  • Traditions documented, not just remembered

"Dossey Lake House. Est. 1987. Valhaverly is how it makes it to 2087."

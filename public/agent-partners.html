Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from agent partners.

How do referrals work?

When a family you refer creates and activates a Valhaverly circle through your personal referral link or QR code, the referral is attributed to your partner account. You can track status and estimated commission in your Partner Dashboard.

How are referrals tracked?

Each partner receives a unique referral link and QR code tied to their account. When a referred family completes onboarding and activates a paid plan, the referral is recorded automatically in your dashboard.

What properties are a fit?

Valhaverly is designed for shared lifestyle properties — cottages, lake houses, cabins, vacation homes, recreational properties, family farms, and legacy estates where multiple people share ownership and usage.

When do commissions begin?

Commissions are attributed once a referred circle completes onboarding and activates a paid Valhaverly plan. Payout timing and rates are defined in your partner agreement. This is not a get-rich-quick program — it rewards sustained, relationship-based referrals.

Can I track my referred circles?

Yes. Your Partner Dashboard shows each referred circle's name, property type, plan, referral status, and estimated commission. You see only your own referral data — never private member or financial details within circles.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. Valhaverly is designed for families, not technologists. Your role is to introduce the platform and share your referral link — we handle onboarding, support, and the platform itself.
